About Lani Macasieb

I am an Information Technology student with a strong passion for UI design and creating user-friendly digital experiences. I enjoy combining creativity and technical skills to design interfaces that are not only visually appealing but also easy to use.

I am continuously learning and developing my skills in design tools such as Figma and Canva, as well as front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript. I pay close attention to detail, color balance, layout, and user experience to ensure that every design I create is clean, modern, and effective.

As a student, I am eager to gain more experience, collaborate with others, and take on new challenges that will help me grow as a UI designer. My goal is to create meaningful digital products that make technology more accessible and enjoyable for everyone.
